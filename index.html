Squirrels are intelligent creatures as they bury their food to mislead humans or any other animal watching them from close by. The burying of food is usually to trick potential stalkers/thieves like birds who know that the squirrel is storing the food in that area


If you want to learn more about the Squirrel, check out its wikipedia page.





Squirrels are awesome because.....

  1. They can also pick up information about their fellow squirrels by smelling them.
  2. They communicate with each other by making sounds and body movements, such as tail flicking.
  3. They do tend to take up other hobbies for leisure and for the necessity of life.
